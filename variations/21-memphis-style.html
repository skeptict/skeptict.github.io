The Memphis Revolution

December 28, 2025
In 1981, a group of Italian designers turned the design world upside down. The Memphis Group rejected minimalism and good taste in favor of bold colors, clashing patterns, and geometric chaos. It was playful, irreverent, and unapologetically loud. Memphis design celebrated kitsch and artifice, proving that design could be fun, expressive, and totally bonkers. Circles, triangles, squiggles, and wild color combinations became the signature of an era.
