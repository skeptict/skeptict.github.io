The Art of Gradient Design

December 28, 2025
Gradients have made a triumphant return to modern web design, moving far beyond the garish effects of the early 2000s. Today's gradients are sophisticated, subtle, and purposeful. They create depth, guide attention, and add visual interest without overwhelming the content. The key is in the transition—smooth, well-chosen color combinations that feel natural and pleasing to the eye.

Animated Backgrounds and User Experience

December 25, 2025
A slowly shifting gradient background can create an engaging, dynamic experience that feels alive. However, subtlety is crucial—animations should enhance rather than distract. The gentle movement keeps the page feeling fresh and modern while maintaining readability through careful use of transparency and contrast.
